The top 5 best Mario games ever

The best rated Mario games from IGN

5. Super Mario 3D Land

Rating:9.5/10

Super Mario 3D Land was considered the best handheld Mario game until the Nintendo Switch arrived, offering a wealth of enjoyable, compact Mario levels and diverse content. It expertly utilized both 3D platforming and nostalgic 2.5D levels, and its use of glasses-free 3D set it apart as a lively gaming experience.

4. Super Mario Sunshine

Rating:9.4/10

Super Mario Sunshine introduced a unique concept of Mario cleaning up a tropical island using the F.L.U.D.D., which added innovative gameplay and vibrant levels. It also brought Yoshi into the 3D platforming world, offering both fun and challenging experiences.

3. Super Mario 64

Rating:9.8/10

Super Mario 64 is celebrated for its enduring playability and innovative 3D design, remaining enjoyable even after 27 years, despite dated graphics. It introduces engaging level design and a versatile physics engine that offers a fun and challenging experience, making it a timeless classic in the world of 3D gaming.

2.Super Mario Odyssey

Rating:10/10

Super Mario Odyssey revitalized the iconic plumber in a globe-trotting adventure, emphasizing a back-to-basics approach with an innovative hat mechanic. Its standout feature is its exceptional gameplay and stunning visuals, reminiscent of the acclaimed Mario 64.

1.Super Mario Galaxy

Rating:9.7/10

If there was one Mario game that exemplified the sheer creativity and sense of wonder that’s at the heart of the series, it’s the first Super Mario Galaxy. Levels went from being just worlds to being galaxies, with tons of planets and open space in between, with each new area offering up their own unique gameplay challenges that pushed the boundaries of what was possible on the Wii hardware.
